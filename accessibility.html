Accessibility Statement

Last Updated: March 14, 2026

ProMax Lending is committed to ensuring digital accessibility for all users, including people with disabilities. We continuously work to improve the user experience for everyone and to apply relevant accessibility standards.

Our Commitment

We strive to maintain a website that is accessible and usable across a broad range of devices, assistive technologies, and browsing conditions.

Accessibility Standards

Our goal is to align with the Web Content Accessibility Guidelines (WCAG) 2.1 Level AA and to make reasonable improvements as standards and technologies evolve.

Ongoing Improvements

Accessibility is an ongoing effort. We regularly review website components, navigation, form interactions, and content readability to improve usability and remove barriers where feasible.

Third-Party Content

Some third-party tools or linked websites may not be fully accessible. While we do not control external content, we welcome feedback on any accessibility issues encountered.
