Loan Purpose

Choose the loan purpose that fits your goals.

Different mortgage goals require different strategies. These common loan purposes help define the right path before choosing a specific program.

Purchase

Designed for homebuyers purchasing a primary residence, second home, or investment property. We help you align budget, down payment, and monthly payment expectations before you make an offer.

Rate & Term Refinance

Focused on improving your current mortgage by lowering your interest rate, changing the loan term, or both. This can help reduce monthly payments or pay off your loan faster depending on your priorities.

Cash-out Refinance

Allows you to refinance and convert a portion of your home equity into cash. Common uses include debt consolidation, home improvements, and funding major expenses while keeping financing in one mortgage.
