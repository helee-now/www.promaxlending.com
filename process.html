Our Process

A structured mortgage workflow built for clarity.

ProMax Lending follows a transparent, milestone-based process so you understand each decision point. From strategy to closing, we align financing recommendations with your timeline, documentation readiness, and long-term goals.

01

Discovery and Goal Alignment

We begin with your purchase, refinance, or investment objective, then define budget range, timeline, and risk tolerance.

02

Qualification Review

Income, assets, credit profile, and property intent are reviewed to identify realistic financing pathways.

03

Program Strategy

We compare appropriate loan structures, rates, terms, and cash-flow tradeoffs to support an informed decision.

04

Documentation and Pre-Approval

Our team guides document collection and pre-approval to strengthen your position before contract or lock decisions.

05

Processing and Coordination

We coordinate with escrow, title, and involved parties to keep underwriting conditions and timelines moving efficiently.

06

Underwriting to Close

Conditions are cleared, final disclosures are reviewed, and we support a smooth signing and funding process through closing.
